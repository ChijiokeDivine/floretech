About Us

We take a distinctly different approach to wealth management. It's a difference that is hard to communicate.
Instead, we invite you to experience it. The company will operate as profitopit.com and have an implied estimated enterprise value of $10.4 billion at closing with $650 million in funding coming from private investment in public equity. Profitopit was founded as RetailFX in 2008.

Where can we help?


We want to understand what matters to you. It’s a question of striking the right balance between your needs now and in the future. As a profit optimizer, we can help you manage your money from day one. As a wealth manager, we can manage your finances and investments over the longer term. As you’d expect from a boutique manager, we treat all our clients as discerning individuals, offering certainty, consistency and flexibility.
